RESTORING ONE DROP AT A TIME

ICE QUEEN WATER SOLUTIONS (IQWS) is a water distribution and treatment company based in South Africa, Johannesburg South and was established recently in January 2025. It falls under the Umbrella of Southern Side Solutions and mainly focuses on the sanitation and distribution of water locally. ​

  • Ice Queen Water Solutions falls under the water utility industry, which is in serious need of effective change in South Africa
  • ICE QUEEN WATER SOLUTIONS offers various products apart from treatments and the distribution of bottled mineral water. We provide an opportunity for South African locals to involve themselves in purifying their own water supply.
  • We provide a 7-year water treatment process which involves the installation of boreholes included. ​
