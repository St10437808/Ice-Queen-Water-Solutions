Our Journey

ICE QUEEN WATER SOLUTIONS (IQWS) is a water distribution and treatment company based in South Africa, Johannesburg South and was established recently in January 2025. It falls under the Umbrella of Southern Side Solutions and mainly focuses on the sanitation and distribution of water locally.

Ice Queen Water Solutions falls under the water utility industry, and offers various products apart from treatments and the distribution of bottled mineral water. We provide an opportunity for South African Locals to involve themselves in purifying and know the importance of reating their own water supply

We've taken the journey of studying various ways households can be independantly confident of one of their most important utility needs which is water. With enough research and further safety considerations Ice Queen Waters was able to uncover a 7 year water treatment process.
